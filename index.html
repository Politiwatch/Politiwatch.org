Politiwatch is an American 501(c)(3) nonpartisan, nonprofit organization that advocates for freedom of information, digital privacy rights, and political accountability.

Our work has been featured in or been referenced by the New York Times, NBC News, Nightly News with Lester Holt, CNN, the Daily Beast, TimeOut, the New York Daily News, and Bustle, among other outlets.

Our flagship projects are PolitiTweet, WhoAreMyRepresentatives.org, and, most recently, PrivacySpy. We also develop a number of experimental projects every year. We're currently working on Atlos.

We're grateful to DigitalOcean and ProtonMail for helping to sponsor our endeavors. If you'd like to contribute, consider supporting our work by donating.

We're looking for new interns and sponsors. To contact us, please send an email to secure@politiwatch.org! We'd love to hear from you.

Politiwatch is an IRS tax-exempt 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, formed in 2016 in New York State (EIN 81-4771872). Politiwatch is managed primarily by Miles McCain.
